About Us & Our Vision

About us, and where this is headed.

We’re a small crew building the kind of graffiti game we used to lose hours in.

If you came up on early 2000s browser games, you already know what we mean. Simple on the surface, weirdly addicting underneath, and built in a way that made you want to keep coming back. That’s the lane we’re in.

What’s live right now is the starting point. The canvas matters, the drawing matters, and getting that part right matters. But the long-term plan is to grow this into something with actual depth — profiles, more interaction, more progression, more reasons for people to plant a flag and stay active.

We’re keeping the deeper details close for now, but this is not the finished product. It’s the first layer.

Just a few graff enthusiasts building a game that feels like something we would’ve been obsessed with twenty years ago.

The Roadmap

  1. 01
    The Canvas Live

    Paint virtual trains, walls, billboards and more — then post your piece to the wall for everyone to see. Getting the drawing right comes first.

  2. 02
    Identity Next

    Profiles and tags. Claim your name, build a presence, and make the wall feel like a place you belong.

  3. 03
    Progression Planned

    Reasons to keep coming back — unlocks, recognition, and a real game loop underneath the painting.

  4. 04
    The Bigger Picture On the horizon

    More interaction, more world, more depth. We’re keeping the details close for now — but this is only the first layer.
